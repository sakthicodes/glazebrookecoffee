Welcome to Glazebrooke Coffee Estate

Brewed by Nature, Perfected by Legacy

Nested in the serene hills of Yercaud, Glaze Brooke Coffee Estate is a 70-year-old legacy of harmony with nature, heritage, and craftsmanship. Situated at altitudes of 3,400 to 4,000 feet, our coffee thrives in a unique microclimate of misty mornings and sunlit afternoons, growing freely in open fields alongside a vibrant ecosystem.

Here, nature is our partner, where bison roam, birds soar, and life flourishes. Every bean reflects this purity, celebrating resilience, biodiversity, and interconnectedness.
